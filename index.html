Alibaba Says New AI Model Is Just Second to Anthropic's Fable 5
At the World AI Conference in Shanghai (around July 19-20, 2026), Alibaba previewed Qwen3.8 (available as Qwen3.8-Max-Preview), a sparse Mixture-of-Experts model with roughly 2.4 trillion parameters that the company calls "second only" to Anthropic's Claude Fable 5 among frontier models. It is Alibaba's first multimodal model to exceed one trillion parameters, handling text, images, video, and documents, and was rolled out on its Token Plan subscription and Qoder/QoderWork agentic platforms. The launch is shadowed by controversy: Anthropic told US senators and White House officials that operators tied to the Qwen lab used nearly 25,000 fraudulent accounts to run about 29 million queries against Claude between April and June 2026, allegedly to distill its software-engineering and agentic reasoning capabilities. Alibaba has not publicly responded to the distillation allegations.
